--- CURSOR PATCH: Inject MetaManager into your <head> ---

1) Add file: /app/lib/MetaManager.php (done in this package). Ensure autoload or require:

   <?php require_once __DIR__.'/../app/lib/MetaManager.php'; ?>

2) In your global head (e.g., /views/partials/head.php or header.php), replace your static <title> and <meta name="description"> with:

   <?php
     $reqPath = parse_url($_SERVER['REQUEST_URI'] ?? '/', PHP_URL_PATH) ?: '/';
     // Your existing defaults:
     $defaultTitle = isset($pageTitle) ? $pageTitle : 'Hoosier Cladding';
     $defaultDesc  = isset($pageDescription) ? $pageDescription : 'Expert siding services across Northern Indiana.';
     $finalTitle = MetaManager::title($reqPath, $defaultTitle);
     $finalDesc  = MetaManager::description($reqPath, $defaultDesc);
   ?>
   <title><?= htmlspecialchars($finalTitle, ENT_QUOTES) ?></title>
   <meta name="description" content="<?= htmlspecialchars($finalDesc, ENT_QUOTES) ?>">

   <!-- keep your other meta tags as-is -->

3) Place the CSV you were given at: /app/config/ctr_rewrites.csv
   (We included a starter one earlier. You can append rows anytime; no code changes needed.)
